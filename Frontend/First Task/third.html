SON GOKU

Kakarot, better known as Son Goku, is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball franchise. Born on Planet Vegeta to a race of powerful alien warriors known as Saiyyans. Goku may be Dragon Ball's main character, but we all know Vegeta is Anime's best hero. Goku and Vegeta are the greatest heroes throughout the Dragon Ball franchise. Goku, the wayward son of Planet Vegeta has dedicated one part of his life to getting stronger and the other to protect all those around him.
